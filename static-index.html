About This Game

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) expands upon the team-based action gameplay that it pioneered when it was launched 19 years ago.

CS:GO features new maps, characters, weapons, and game modes, and delivers updated versions of the classic CS content (de_dust2, etc.).

CS:GO has remained one of the most-played games in the world, continuing to headline competitive gaming tournaments and selling over 25 million units worldwide across the franchise.

Join the global CS:GO community and experience the evolution of the world's most popular tactical FPS.

System Requirements

MINIMUM:

  • OS: Windows® 7/Vista/XP
  • Processor: Intel® Core™ 2 Duo E6600 or AMD Phenom™ X3 8750 processor or better
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Video card must be 256 MB or more and should be a DirectX 9-compatible with support for Pixel Shader 3.0
  • DirectX: Version 9.0c
  • Storage: 15 GB available space

RECOMMENDED:

  • OS: Windows® 8.1
  • Processor: Intel® Core™ i5-3330 or better
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Video card must be 1GB VRAM GDDR4 or DDR3, NVIDIA® GeForce™ GTX 770
  • DirectX: Version 10.0
  • Storage: 30 GB available space
