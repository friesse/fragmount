How to Check Your Best Server

You can check your ping to CS:GO servers using the in-game settings. Follow these steps:

  1. Open CS:GO
  2. Click on "Settings" in the main menu
  3. Select the "Game Settings" tab
  4. Click on "Data Centers" under "Matchmaking"
  5. Wait for the ping test to complete

The lower the ping value, the better your connection to that server. For optimal gameplay experience, choose servers with ping below 60ms when possible.
